Method

How the score is built.

  • Price drop after market-moving rhetoric or policy threats
  • Support when affected tickers stabilize instead of cascading
  • Bonus when later news implies delay, carve-out, denial, or softer tone
  • Extra weight for broad-market ETFs and exposed sectors
  • Penalty when escalation continues with no reversal signal
